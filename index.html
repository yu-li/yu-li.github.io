I am currently a Principal Researcher at International Digital Economy Academy (IDEA), where I lead Vistring Lab and focus on video content generation and editing. Before joining IDEA, I was Lead of Video Generation Features at Tencent ARC Lab, and Lead of DaVinci AI Neural Engine Features at Blackmagic Design. I got my PhD degree from National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Michael S Brown. I was a PostDoc Researcher at UIUC Advanced Digital Science Center. I am also an adjunct professor at the South China University of Technology and CUHK(SZ).
